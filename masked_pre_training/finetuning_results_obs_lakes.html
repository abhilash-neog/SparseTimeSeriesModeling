Training Procedure:

  • Each lake was split into 60:40
  • 3 Models were pretrained on the 60% of data of each lake with following mask ratios: 25, 50, 75
  • Once pretrained, each model was used to finetune on each lake (60% portion of it) and tested on the remaining 40%

Next steps/experiments:

1. A good next experiment would be to try zero-shot on each lake and compare the results of fine-tuning
2. Another experiment would be to use the pre-trained models and apply them on lakes that were never part of the training set (not even it's 60%)
3. Compare these performances with scratch LSTM and Transformer models
4. Compare these performances with LSTM and Transformer models trained on all lakes (with a similar configuration as this)
